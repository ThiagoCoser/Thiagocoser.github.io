Forest

The flora of a real time virtual forest-garden, full of recognizable, affective and alien plants disappears over time, as it perceives the proximity of some human presence. If the detection process is prolonged, at the end of the process, there is only the silence of the Cartesian plane that forms the infertile soil, reinforcing the artificiality of the simulacrum. If no human presence is detected, the flora gradually recomposes itself.
